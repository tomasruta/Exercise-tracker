## DOUBLE PROGRESSION (ALL exercises)

**Compounds (6-8 rep range):** squats, pull-ups, dips, rows, presses, RDL
- Top of range on ALL sets → +1-2.5kg → drop to bottom of range

**Isolation (8-12 rep range):** curls, tricep ext, Powell, ext rot
- Same rule

**ATG-specific:** back ext, QL, reverse squat, rhythm squat — keep original reps, add load when form solid
